Lab: 構造化知能研究室


From 2025.04.01, I join Hokkaido University (北海道大学) as an associate professor. From 2023.10.01-2025.03.31, I was a Project Assistant professor at CVM (Yamasaki Lab), The University of Tokyo (東京大学), Japan. From 2021.06 to 2023.10, I was a Project Researcher at CVM (Yamasaki Lab), The University of Tokyo (東京大学), Japan. In 2020.12, I received my Ph.D. degree at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (华中科技大学), P.R. China. My research interests focus on 1) theoretical research: representation learning, semantic segmentation, edge detection, small object detection, feature detection and matching, self-supervised learning, mutual learning, continue learning, open-set object detection, reading comprehension, graph architectures for giant graph, video summarization, and layout generation. 2) application research: anomaly detection, fashion recommendation, and intelligent manufacturing. From 2018.10-to 2019.11, I was a visiting student at the University of Queensland, Australia. From 2014.07 to 2015.08, I was a mechanical engineer at BYD Company Limited, P. R. China.
