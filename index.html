IEEE Senior Member

From 2025.04.01, I join Hokkaido University as an associate professor. From 2023.10.01–2025.03.31, I was a Project Assistant Professor at CVM (Yamasaki Lab), The University of Tokyo, Japan. From 2021.06 to 2023.10, I was a Project Researcher at CVM (Yamasaki Lab), The University of Tokyo, Japan. In 2020.12, I received my Ph.D. degree at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, P.R. China. From 2018.10 to 2019.11, I was a visiting student at The University of Queensland, Australia. From 2014.07 to 2015.08, I was a mechanical engineer at BYD Company Limited, P.R. China.

My research interests focus on multimedia signal processing for intelligent perception and decision-making, efficient multimodal language models, robotic navigations, fine-grained representation learning, continual learning, open-world recognition, adversarial attacks, etc..
